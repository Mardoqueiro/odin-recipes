Chicken & Mayo Sandwich

This is an image of a Chicken & Mayo Sandwich

Below is the unordered list of the ingredient needed for the recipe.

  • 1 store-bought barbecue chicken
  • 235 g (1 cup) mayonnaise
  • salt and black pepper, to taste
  • 1 loaf sliced white sandwich bread
  • salted spreadable butter
  • Jam
  • Jam

Ordered list of the steps needed for making the dish.

  • Take the chicken meat off the bone, discard the skin and bones and shred the meat into a bowl. Add the mayonnaise and season to taste.
  • pread the bread slices with butter. Top half the bread with the chicken mixture, then sandwich together with remaining bread slices. Using a serrated knife, remove the crusts and cut each sandwich lengthways into thirds.
