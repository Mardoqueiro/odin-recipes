Cheese Sandwich

This is an image of a cheese sandwich

Below is the unordered list of the ingredient needed for the recipe.

  • 4 Tbsp. unsalted butter, softened
  • 8 slices whole grain bread
  • 2 Cups Shredded Parmesan Cheese
  • 8 slices Natural American Cheese
  • 4 slices Muenster Cheese

Ordered list of the steps needed for making the dish.

  • Spread butter mixture evenly on one side of each slice of bread.
  • Sprinkle 1/2 cup Sargento® Parmesan Cheese into non-stick skillet or griddle over medium low heat. Place two slices of bread, butter side down, over Parmesan cheese.
  • Place one slice of Sargento® Natural American Cheese over each slice of bread. Top one slice of bread with one slice of Sargento® Muenster Cheese. Continue to cook for 2-3 minutes.
  • Combine each half of grilled cheese to make one sandwich. Continue cooking until bread is golden brown.
